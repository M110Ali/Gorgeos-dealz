    Does a higher price always indicate better quality?

    No, not always. The quality of a product or service is just one of many factors that can influence the price of a product or service.

    Other factors that can influence a product's or service's price include the cost of raw materials, manufacturing processes, the demand for the product or service, market competition, and the value the product or service provides to the consumer.
